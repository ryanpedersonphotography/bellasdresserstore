Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Shopping at Bella's

Tourist & Visitor Information

I'm visiting from Breezy Point Resort. How far is your boutique?

We're just 15 minutes from Breezy Point Resort! Take County Road 11 north to Highway 371, then follow 371 north for about 8 miles. Turn right on Patriot Ave into downtown Pequot Lakes. You'll find us on Government Drive with easy parking right out front.

Do you offer any discounts for tourists visiting the Brainerd Lakes area?

Yes! We love our lake area visitors. Mention which resort or lake you're visiting from, and we'll be happy to offer you our tourist appreciation discount. We know you have many shopping choices in the area, and we're grateful you chose Bella's Dresser!

Can I shop at Bella's if I'm just passing through Pequot Lakes?

Absolutely! Many of our customers discover us while exploring the shops of downtown Pequot Lakes. We're right on Government Drive with plenty of parking, making it easy to stop in even if you're just driving through on Highway 371.

What other shopping is nearby in downtown Pequot Lakes?

Downtown Pequot Lakes offers charming antique shops, gift boutiques, art galleries, and several restaurants all within walking distance. Make a day of it! We're happy to hold your purchases while you explore the other shops.

Shopping & Sizing

What dress sizes do you carry?

We stock a wide range of sizes from XS to 3X, though availability varies by style. Our collection includes petite, regular, and plus sizes. If we don't have your size in stock, we're often able to special order it for you.

Do you offer alterations?

While we don't provide in-house alterations, we work with several talented local seamstresses in the Pequot Lakes and Brainerd area. We're happy to provide recommendations and can often arrange for simple hemming services.

Can I special order a dress I saw online?

Yes! If you've seen a specific dress online or in a magazine, bring us the details. We work with many designers and can often special order styles not currently in our boutique. Special orders typically take 2-4 weeks.

Store Policies

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days with original tags and receipt. Items must be unworn and in original condition. Sale items and special orders are final sale. We understand tourists may need flexibility - just let us know your situation!

Do you ship purchases?

Yes! We're happy to ship your purchases anywhere in the United States. This is perfect for tourists who don't want to pack formal dresses in their lake vacation luggage. Shipping rates vary by location and package size.

Do you accept phone orders?

Absolutely! If you've shopped with us before or saw something you loved, call us at (218) 568-4783. We can process payment over the phone and ship directly to you - perfect for Gull Lake cabin owners who can't make it to town!

Is there parking available?

Yes! Free parking is available right in front of our boutique on Government Drive. Additional free parking can be found throughout downtown Pequot Lakes, all within a short walk.

Planning a Visit to the Brainerd Lakes Area?

Whether you're staying at Breezy Point, vacationing on Gull Lake, or exploring Crosslake and Nisswa, make Bella's Dresser part of your shopping adventure. We're your boutique dress destination in the heart of lake country!
