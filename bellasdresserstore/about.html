About Bella's Dresser

Your Story Begins with the Perfect Dress

Our Boutique Story

Nestled in the heart of downtown Pequot Lakes, Bella's Dresser has been dressing women for life's special moments since we opened our doors. What started as a dream to bring curated fashion to the Brainerd Lakes area has blossomed into a beloved destination for locals and tourists alike.

Just minutes from the resorts of Gull Lake and Breezy Point, our boutique has become a must-visit shopping stop for women vacationing in the area. Whether you're searching for the perfect summer dress to wear at Crosslake's waterfront restaurants or need a stunning gown for a resort wedding, Bella's Dresser offers a carefully selected collection that celebrates femininity and style.

Why Tourists Love Shopping at Bella's

Visitors from across Minnesota and beyond discover us while exploring downtown Pequot Lakes. Our location on Government Drive puts us at the center of the area's charming shopping district, making us the perfect addition to your lake country getaway. Many of our customers tell us that finding Bella's Dresser was the highlight of their Nisswa and Pequot Lakes shopping adventure.

We specialize in resort wear that's perfect for the Brainerd Lakes lifestyle - from breezy sundresses ideal for boat days on Gull Lake to elegant evening wear for special dinners at Breezy Point Resort. Our collection is thoughtfully curated to offer something special you won't find in big city department stores.

More Than Just a Dress Shop

At Bella's Dresser, we believe every woman deserves to feel beautiful. Our boutique atmosphere provides a personal shopping experience that online retailers simply can't match. Whether you're a Pequot Lakes local preparing for prom or a tourist from the Twin Cities looking for that perfect vacation outfit, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find exactly what you're looking for.
