Top Summer Dress Styles in the Brainerd Lakes Area

By Bella's Team | Summer Fashion

Summer in the Brainerd Lakes area calls for versatile dresses that transition seamlessly from boat days to dinner dates. This season, we're seeing tourists and locals embrace these must-have styles:

1. Flowy Maxi Dresses: Perfect for strolling through downtown Nisswa or attending outdoor concerts at Gull Lake. The longer length provides sun protection while keeping you cool.

2. Wrap Dresses: Flattering on everyone and ideal for resort dining. Our customers staying at Breezy Point love how these pack easily and resist wrinkles.

3. Sundresses with Pockets: Because who doesn't love pockets? Great for carrying your phone while shopping in Pequot Lakes or exploring Crosslake's art fair.
