By Bella Dubois | Shopping Guide

When I first arrived from Lyon, I was enchanted by this charming Main Street that reminded me of the boutique districts in small French towns. The intimate scale, the personal service, the way shop owners know your name - c'est merveilleux!

Today, I want to be your personal guide through our delightful downtown. Whether you're a tourist exploring the Brainerd Lakes area or a local looking for hidden gems, this insider's tour will help you discover the best of Pequot Lakes shopping, dining, and experiences.

Start Your Day the French Way

Begin with caf√© au lait at our local coffee shop - I insist on proper coffee before any shopping expedition! In France, we never rush into the day. Take a moment to plan your route, people-watch, and prepare for the treasures ahead.

‚òï Bella's Morning Ritual:

I start every day at 7:30 AM with coffee and a pastry while I plan which new dresses to display. By 9 AM, I'm arranging the boutique, and by 10 AM, I'm ready to help you find your perfect outfit. The early morning energy on Main Street is magical!

The Shopping District Layout

Downtown Pequot Lakes is wonderfully walkable - everything you need is within a few charming blocks. Government Drive (where you'll find Bella's Dresser) intersects with Main Avenue, creating our cozy shopping district. The beauty? You can park once and explore everything on foot!

Key Shopping Areas:

Government Drive: Home to Bella's Dresser and specialty shops
Main Avenue: Cafes, gift shops, and local treasures
Sibley Street: Antiques and home decor

Must-Visit Shops & Boutiques

Bella's Dresser (Naturally!)

0976 Government Dr | Fashion & Dresses

Forgive my bias, but we must start here! Naturellement, I've curated pieces that blend French sophistication with Minnesota practicality. From summer sundresses perfect for lake days to elegant evening wear for resort dinners, we dress the women of the Brainerd Lakes area with style and grace.

Local Artisan Galleries

Various locations on Main Ave

Our local artists capture the beauty of the lakes in ways that make my French heart sing! Find handcrafted jewelry that pairs perfectly with our dresses, paintings of local landscapes, and unique pottery. I often recommend customers pair a new dress with locally made accessories - c'est parfait!

Antique Treasures

Downtown area

The antique shops here rival the flea markets of Paris! I've found vintage brooches that transform a simple dress into something special, and antique mirrors that now grace my boutique. Perfect for treasure hunters and those who appreciate history.

Gift & Home Decor Shops

Main Avenue

These charming shops offer everything from Minnesota-made products to lake-themed home decor. When friends visit from France, this is where I find the perfect "taste of Minnesota" gifts to send home with them.

Shopping requires sustenance! Our downtown offers delightful dining options that complement your shopping adventure:

Seasonal Shopping Events

Spring: Sidewalk Sales & Garden Tours

When the snow melts, our sidewalks bloom with special sales and displays. I love setting up racks of colorful spring dresses outside - it brings such life to the street!

Summer: Art Fairs & Tourist Season

Peak season brings visitors from around the world. The energy is infectious! Extended hours, special events, and the chance to meet people from everywhere. I've dressed ladies from Paris to Tokyo right here on Government Drive.

Fall: Harvest Festivals & Cozy Shopping

My favorite season! The crisp air, the changing leaves, and shoppers looking for transitional pieces. We host "Sip & Shop" events with local wine and cider.

Winter: Holiday Magic & Gift Giving

Downtown transforms into a winter wonderland. Twinkling lights, holiday music, and the joy of finding the perfect gift. I special-order velvet and sequined dresses for holiday parties.

Shopping Tips from a Local

üí° Best Shopping Times:

Weekday mornings are gloriously peaceful - perfect for serious shopping with personal attention. Saturdays bring energy and excitement. Avoid peak summer weekends if you prefer a quieter experience, or embrace the bustle if you love people-watching!

üöó Parking Secrets:

There's ample free parking throughout downtown. My favorite spot is behind the shops on Government Drive - always spaces available and you're steps from everything!
