Bella's Favorite Places Around Pequot

By Bella Dubois | Local Guide

Mes chéris, after living in this enchanting corner of Minnesota for several years now, I've discovered the most delightful spots that remind me why I fell in love with the Brainerd Lakes area. When friends visit from France or when customers ask for recommendations, these are the treasures I always share!

Let me take you on a little journey to my favorite places around Pequot Lakes - the spots that capture both the charm of small-town Minnesota and the joie de vivre that makes life here so special.

🏨 Grand View Lodge - My Crown Jewel

Grand View Lodge, Spa & Golf Resort

Location: Nisswa (just minutes from Pequot Lakes)
What makes it special: C'est magnifique! This resort reminds me of the grand hotels of the French Riviera, but with that unmistakable Minnesota warmth.

Since 1916, Grand View Lodge has been creating memories on beautiful Gull Lake, and I must say, they've perfected the art! With 750 acres of pine forest and 2,500 feet of pristine sandy beach, it's where I take all my special guests.

Why I adore it: Eight different restaurants on property means there's always somewhere new to discover. From romantic dinners at Char Restaurant to casual fun at Northwoods Pub, it's like having a little village of dining experiences!

Bella's tip: Book dinner at CRU Restaurant & Wine Bar during sunset - the view over Gull Lake with a glass of wine? Pure magic, mes amis!

🍽️ My Beloved Local Dining Spots

When I'm not cooking coq au vin at home, you'll find me at these charming local establishments. Each one has captured a piece of my heart!

Lucky's Tavern

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highly Rated by Locals)

Cuisine: American & Irish
Why I love it: The atmosphere here reminds me of the cozy bistros in Lyon - warm, welcoming, and full of laughter. Their Irish influence adds a delightful twist that pairs perfectly with good conversation and even better company!

Perfect for: Casual dinner with friends or a fun evening out

A-Pine Family Restaurant

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Famous for Their Pies!)

Cuisine: Classic American Diner
Why it's special: Mon Dieu, their pies! As someone who appreciates good pastry, I must say their pie selection rivals anything I've had in a Parisian patisserie. This is exactly what you hope for from a charming roadside diner.

Bella's order: Whatever the daily special is, followed by their famous pie - trust me on this!

Lakes Tavern

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Beautiful Patio!)

Cuisine: Fresh American with Scratch Kitchen
What sets it apart: Their commitment to fresh ingredients and scratch cooking speaks to my French sensibilities. Plus, their 60-seat patio with fire features is absolutely divine for evening dining!

Perfect for: Romantic dinners on the patio or celebrating special occasions

Bella's Secret to Perfect Dining

"The best meals aren't just about the food - they're about the stories you create!"

I always ask my server for their personal favorite dish. In small-town Minnesota, the staff often eat where they work, so their recommendations are toujours parfait - always perfect!

🎯 Other Pequot Lakes Gems Worth Discovering

Main Street Ale House

Perfect for casual dining and craft beer lovers. The local atmosphere here is authentique - you'll feel like a regular after your first visit!

Barajas Mexican Bar and Grill

When I'm craving something spicy and vibrant, this is my go-to spot. The flavors are bold and exciting - a delightful change from traditional Minnesota fare!

🌲 What Makes These Places Special

You know what I love most about all these spots? They each capture something that drew me to this area - the genuine warmth of Minnesota hospitality combined with attention to quality that reminds me of the best French establishments.

Whether it's the grand elegance of Grand View Lodge or the cozy charm of A-Pine's famous pies, each place tells a story of people who care about their craft and their community. C'est exactement what I was looking for when I left France to start my boutique here!

🍾 My Perfect Pequot Lakes Day

Here's how I like to spend a perfect day exploring our area:

  • Morning: Coffee and pastry at a local café, then shopping (of course, starting at Bella's Dresser! Naturellement!)
  • Afternoon: Lunch at A-Pine Family Restaurant with their famous pie for dessert
  • Late Afternoon: Stroll around Grand View Lodge's beautiful grounds and beach
  • Evening: Dinner at one of Grand View's restaurants or Lakes Tavern's gorgeous patio
  • Night: Cocktails and conversation at Lucky's Tavern

🎨 The French Connection

What amazes me most is how these Minnesota destinations capture the same qualities I loved about my favorite places in France - attention to detail, pride in craftsmanship, and the understanding that good food and beautiful settings bring people together.

Grand View Lodge has that timeless elegance of a grand European hotel. Lucky's Tavern has the warm conviviality of a French bistro. A-Pine serves pie with the same passion that a Parisian baker approaches his croissants. C'est merveilleux!

📍 Planning Your Own Pequot Lakes Adventure

Whether you're a tourist discovering our area for the first time or a local who hasn't explored all these gems yet, I encourage you to visit each of these special places. Take your time, savor the experience, and most importantly - enjoy the journey!

And of course, when you're planning what to wear for your dining adventures, stop by Bella's Dresser first. I'll help you put together the perfect outfit for any occasion - from casual lakeside dining to elegant resort evenings. After all, mes chéries, when you look good, you feel good, and when you feel good, everything tastes better!

Here's to discovering the magic in our own backyard!
