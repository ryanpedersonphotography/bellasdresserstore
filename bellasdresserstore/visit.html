Your Guide to Finding Us in Downtown Pequot Lakes

Directions from Popular Lakes Area Destinations

From Breezy Point Resort

15 minutes (11 miles)

Take County Road 11 north to Highway 371. Turn right (north) on 371 and continue for 8 miles. Turn right on Patriot Ave into downtown Pequot Lakes. We're on Government Drive, just off Main Street.

From Gull Lake Dam

12 minutes (9 miles)

Head north on County Road 77 to Highway 371. Turn left (north) on 371 for 3 miles. Turn right on Patriot Ave into Pequot Lakes. Find us on Government Drive in the heart of downtown.

From Crosslake

10 minutes (8 miles)

Take County Road 3 east to Highway 371. Turn left (north) on 371 for about 5 miles. Turn right on Patriot Ave. Bella's Dresser is located on Government Drive downtown.

From Nisswa

20 minutes (15 miles)

Head north on Highway 371 through Pequot Lakes. Turn right on Patriot Ave into downtown. We're conveniently located on Government Drive with easy parking.

From Brainerd

25 minutes (22 miles)

Take Highway 371 north through Baxter and past Nisswa. Continue to Pequot Lakes and turn right on Patriot Ave. Find us in downtown on Government Drive.

From Grand View Lodge

18 minutes (14 miles)

Head south on County Road 77 to Highway 371. Turn right (north) on 371. In Pequot Lakes, turn right on Patriot Ave to reach downtown and Government Drive.

Easy Downtown Parking

Free parking is available right in front of our boutique on Government Drive! Additional parking can be found throughout downtown Pequot Lakes, all within a short walk of Bella's Dresser.

Tourist Shopping Tips for the Brainerd Lakes Area

  • Visit us Tuesday-Friday for the most personalized service - weekends can be busy with fellow tourists!
  • Combine your visit with lunch at a downtown Pequot Lakes restaurant for the perfect girls' day out
  • We're happy to hold your purchases while you explore other downtown shops
  • Ask about our tourist discount when you mention which resort or lake you're visiting from
  • Follow Highway 371's "Lakes Area Shopping Trail" signs to discover more boutiques between Nisswa and Pequot Lakes
  • Summer Saturdays feature sidewalk sales and special events in downtown Pequot Lakes
