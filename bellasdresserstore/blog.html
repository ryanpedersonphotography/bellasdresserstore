Top Summer Dress Styles in the Brainerd Lakes Area

By Bella Dubois | Summer Fashion

Bonjour mes chéries! As a French fashionista who has fallen in love with the magical Brainerd Lakes area, I am absolutely enchanted by how our summer styles can capture both European elegance and Minnesota lake life charm.

1. Flowing Maxi Dresses: Magnifique! These remind me of the summer dresses we wear in Provence, but perfect for your boat days on Gull Lake. The way they dance in the lake breeze while you stroll through downtown Nisswa? Pure poetry, darlings!

2. Wrap Dresses: Ah, the classic wrap! This silhouette has graced French women for decades because it is universally flattering. When you're dining at Breezy Point Resort, you want something chic yet comfortable - the wrap dress delivers both with that perfect je ne sais quoi.

3. Sundresses with Pockets: You Americans and your obsession with pockets! But I must admit, it's rather practical. Perfect for carrying your phone while discovering the charming boutiques of Pequot Lakes or browsing Crosslake's delightful art fair.

Wedding Guest Dresses for Lakes Area Venues

By Bella Dubois | Wedding Fashion

Mes amours, wedding guest dressing is an art form! In France, we have a saying: "Il faut être élégante sans éclipser la mariée" - be elegant without overshadowing the bride. This philosophy has served me well at château weddings and now here at these magical lakeside celebrations.

For outdoor ceremonies at Grand View Lodge or along Gull Lake's shores, choose flowing fabrics that dance with the breeze - think chiffon, silk, or high-quality cotton blends. Midi lengths are parfait for navigating grass and docks while maintaining elegance. Et voilà! You'll be comfortable and chic!
