Welcome to Bella's Dresser

Located in the heart of charming downtown Pequot Lakes, Bella's Dresser is your go-to destination for dresses for every occasion. From casual sundresses perfect for strolling the boardwalk to elegant evening wear for special events, we've curated a collection that celebrates every moment of your life.

Conveniently situated on Government Drive with plenty of easy parking right out front, we're just a short walk from downtown's delightful restaurants, cafes, and shops. Whether you're a local or visiting our beautiful lakes area, make Bella's Dresser part of your perfect day in downtown Pequot Lakes. Come as you are – leave dressed to impress!
